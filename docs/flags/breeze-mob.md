# Breeze Mob

**Property:** `MC_DEBUG_BREEZE_MOB`
**Tab:** Renderers
**Requires restart:** Yes

## Description

Shows the Breeze mob's internal state as an overlay, including its current charge phase, the trajectory of any wind charges it has launched, and the targets that wind charges are deflecting toward. This gives a frame-by-frame view of the Breeze's attack cycle and projectile behavior. Deflection targets are drawn as lines from the wind charge to the entity being aimed at.

## When to use

Enable this when implementing or debugging Breeze AI behaviors, wind charge deflection logic, or verifying that charge phase transitions occur at the correct points in the attack animation.
